Hello and Welcome on my Website!

My name is Sabrina Schekerka and I am currently training to become a web designer. Looking for a new challenge and more flexibility in terms of location I came across this great profession in which I learn to design digital interfaces user-friendly and creative.

As my main goal I aim for a freelance job and would like to work in the fields of renewable energies, non-profit start-ups or website support for bands and artists.

My greatest passion is understanding creative nuances to drive cross-cultural advertising and in fact, I see myself more as a “forever student” who is focused on steadily building creative foundations in web design.

I firmly believe patience and mindfulness in the workspace are the keys to personal growth, a tenet I lived out through my interests in playing music and painting.
